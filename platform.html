Core modules

The operating layer behind your club

Every module is designed to reduce manual admin, connect records and give club leaders a clearer view of day-to-day operations.

Member management

Keep a complete, up-to-date record of every member, their role, section and subscription status.

Availability & team selection

Collect availability, select teams and announce squads — all from one place.

Match fees & subscriptions

Manage match fees, membership subscriptions and payment reminders with clear tracking.

Fixtures & events

Organise fixtures, social events and club activities in a shared calendar.

Club communications

Send targeted updates to teams, sections or the whole club, including connected WhatsApp communication workflows for admins.

Finance workflows

Track income, manage purchase orders and give treasurers visibility of club finances.

Mobile App

Your whole club in your pocket

A clean, role-aware mobile experience designed for every member. Whether you're a player marking availability, a captain selecting a squad, or an admin running operations — the app surfaces exactly what you need.

Events & Availability
  • Receive event invitations and RSVP with a single tap
  • View upcoming fixtures, training sessions and club events in one timeline
  • Real-time availability tracking — available, unavailable or maybe
  • Automated availability request notifications sent at configurable windows before events
  • Auto-sync fixtures from Play Cricket and other sports federation platforms
Team Selection & Announcements
  • Captains and admins select squads directly in-app from available members
  • Team announcements pushed to selected players instantly
  • Full WhatsApp integration — send squad sheets and match details to team groups
  • Google Maps integration embedded in every event for one-tap directions to venues
  • Match-day role assignments, kit numbers and captain designations
Payments & Member Profile
  • View and pay match fees directly in-app
  • Annual subscription status, payment history and outstanding balances
  • Member profile with section, group, team and role information
  • Admins get the same app with elevated views and management controls

Admin UI

Complete control for committees

The admin web UI gives secretaries, treasurers, captains and committee members a single, structured place to run every club operation with confidence and full visibility.

Membership & Subscriptions
  • Full member registry with roles, sections, groups and contact details
  • Subscription type management — annual, seasonal, junior, life member
  • Renewal tracking with automatic status updates and expiry alerts
  • Bulk member import, onboarding flows and offboarding records
  • Role and permission assignment per section, group or team
Financial Workflows
  • Match fee collection with automatic allocation per event and team
  • Subscription payment tracking with outstanding balance visibility
  • Revenue reconciliation reports by month, section or payment type
  • Purchase Order workflow — raise, approve and track club expenses
  • Treasurer dashboard with income, expenditure and surplus summary
  • Export-ready financial records for committee review and audits
Operations & Reporting
  • Club-wide activity overview — events, attendance and availability trends
  • Section and group level reporting for section leads
  • Configurable roles so captains, treasurers and secretaries each see their remit

Structure & permissions

Manage the club as it actually operates

Many clubs are not a flat list of members. Avial is designed around a flexible Club -> Sections -> Groups -> Teams hierarchy, with roles and permissions layered around the structure.

Your club can define sections, groups and teams in the way that best supports its operations rather than being forced into a fixed template.

In practice this means Club -> Sections -> Groups -> Teams, with administration delegated to the right role at each level.

Club AdminConfigure the club, reporting, members, subscriptions and permissions.
Section LeadManage section members, teams, communications and section reporting.
Group CoordinatorCoordinate group availability, messages, records and activity updates.
Team CaptainSelect squads, track match availability and message their team.
MemberSee and act only on the relevant club, section, group or team workflows.
