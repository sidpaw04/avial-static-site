Club structure

Built for fully configurable club structures

Avial lets clubs design their operating structure around how they actually work: Club → Sections → Groups → Teams.

Sections can represent sports or operational areas such as cricket, tennis, bar & events or facilities. Groups can then reflect men’s, women’s, juniors, social or any structure the club chooses.
